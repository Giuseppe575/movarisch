MOVARISCH terms of use

This software is owned by Dr. Giuseppe Strifezza. Usage is restricted to authorised consultants and collaborators for internal chemical risk evaluation activities.

Key terms

  • The application can run offline on managed corporate devices.
  • Redistribution to unauthorised third parties is forbidden without written approval from the owner.
  • Processed data stays on the client side and is never uploaded to external servers.

Available licensing models

Depending on operational needs and compliance requirements, MOVARISCH can be licensed under the following models.

Per-user licence (seat-based)

  • Fixed number of named users with personal credentials.
  • Login required with activity tracking for audit purposes.
  • Ideal for consultancy teams or laboratories with clearly assigned roles.

Per-device licence (node-locked)

  • Activation key bound to the hardware fingerprint of the device.
  • Managed transfer available on request, e.g. when replacing a workstation.
  • Suited for dedicated stations or shared environments.

Term: subscription or perpetual

  • Subscription: monthly or annual renewals with updates and support included for the full term.
  • Perpetual: indefinite right to use; updates and maintenance delivered for a defined period (e.g. 3 years) with optional renewals.

Functional editions

  • Standard: core SDS/TDS parsing and risk index calculation capabilities.
  • Pro: adds advanced reporting, custom exports and audit trail features.
  • Enterprise: enables API integrations, Single Sign-On and multi-site deployments.
  • The active edition is enforced via feature flags embedded in the licence payload.
